ABOUT US

Pistacho is a family business that we created in 2022. We are four members: two cousins with their mothers.
The four of us are very enterprising women since we were little girls and we had the beautiful idea of coming together to create this brand that we love so much.
We are passionate about what we do and share our love for handbags. When it comes to bags, our motto is always the same: “They are NEVER enough!”.
Today we maintain our motto and share it with you.
Thank you to all our loyal clients for trusting us and continuing to choose us.
